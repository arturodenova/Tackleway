The Property

Your home away
from home

Tucked away on the historic Tackleway in Hastings Old Town, The Blue House is a lovingly restored coastal cottage offering everything you need for a perfect seaside escape. You'll be in love the moment you arrive.

Wake up to the sound of the sea, explore the winding Old Town lanes, and return each evening to a cosy, stylishly furnished retreat. Whether you're here for a weekend break or a longer stay, The Blue House has you covered.
